ABOUT US

At Carthage, we simplify your saving and optimize your investing. We empower our members to save consistently while earning strong, reliable returns on their investments. Our investment strategy includes both U.S. Dollars and Kenya Shilling denominated assets, creating a resilient foundation for sustainable wealth growth that stands strong through any market.

INVESTMENT STRATEGY

Long term Investment Philosophy

Our investment approach is rooted in disciplined, long-term value creation. We acquire high-quality businesses—locally and internationally—at fair valuations and hold them for sustained growth and stable returns. This allows us to benefit from compounding while maintaining a conservative risk profile.

Strategic Capital Allocation

When attractive equity opportunities are limited, we deploy capital into secure, income-generating assets such as treasury bills, government bonds, and investment-grade corporate debt. This ensures consistent liquidity, capital preservation, and stable returns across market cycles.

Proven Perfomance & Risk Management

Our diversified and methodical strategy has consistently delivered competitive, risk-adjusted returns. Over time, it has outperformed many traditional asset classes while safeguarding investor capital.
